**Series: Beware the Beginnings – Reclaiming Our Local Narrative**

Inspired by the [Australia Institute's 'A Time for Bravery'](https://australiainstitute.org.au/store/a-time-for-bravery), we believe that the biggest risk in the next election isn't change—it is more of the same. 

For decades, the "party-line" approach has meant that local voices are often checked at the door of Parliament House. When a representative is bound by a party machine, they must vote with that machine 100% of the time, even when it conflicts with the interests of our community. 

### The Power of the Crossbench

Across Australia, we are seeing what happens when communities choose bravery over "safe" party seats. Independent representatives are demonstrating that one person, answerable only to their electorate, can achieve more than a backbencher in a major party.

**David Pocock** has shown that an independent can be a "power vote" rather than just a protest vote. In his first term, he has consistently forced the government to negotiate on housing, climate action, and transparency. He has used his position to advocate for a ten-year National Housing and Homelessness Plan and has been a tireless voice for integrity in government.

**Helen Haines** in the seat of Indi has proven that a community independent can drive national policy. She championed the fight for a federal integrity commission for years, eventually forcing the creation of the National Anti-Corruption Commission. She also developed the "Cheaper Home Batteries" program—a policy that started in her local community and is now a $415 million national initiative.

### Why Boothby is Ready

Boothby is a marginal electorate with a history of close contests. In 2022, we saw a significant appetite for alternatives, with voters increasingly looking for representation that isn't beholden to party bosses or major donors.

We don't need a representative who answers to a factional leader in Canberra. We need someone who:
1.  **Values Integrity over Donations:** Someone who refuses money from the fossil fuel lobby and property developers, freeing them to vote for the community's interests.
2.  **Has the Courage to Speak Truth:** Someone who will stand in Parliament and ask the hard questions about why our resources are being given away for free while local families struggle with the cost of living.
3.  **Builds Community Policy:** An independent who works with local experts—from our healthcare workers at Flinders to our environmental groups in the hills—to develop solutions tailored to Boothby.

### Be Part of the Change

"Beware the beginnings" of a better future starts with the realization that the major parties don't own your vote. In 2025, over a million Australians chose independent candidates—a clear signal that the old way of doing politics is no longer enough.

We are looking for a local leader who will take this spirit of independence to Canberra. The time for bravery is now. Join us in finding a better voice for Boothby.
