Leo Gaggl is a passionate advocate for community-led change and independent representation in the Australian Federal Electorate of Boothby. With a background in community organising and a deep understanding of local issues, Leo is committed to fostering a more transparent and accountable political landscape. He believes that true representation comes from listening to and empowering the community, not from adhering to party lines.
